About Me

Hi! I'm Toby and I guess this is hello. I would consider myself a motivated, organised, hardworking Bsc (hons) Computer Science Undergraduate from the University of Lincoln with a great attitude towards working and learning in a team. I possess an intimate understanding of programming fundamentals and have a passion for software development and web design. I have done my best to develop these skills and passion through university projects as well as many projects that I've undertook in my own time. I have recently dedicated a lot of my time to delving into the worlds of website design and to teaching. I have been creating websites and blogs for businesses from scratch by using various frameworks as well as creating the online course “Code_Otter” to help educate newcomers to the world of python and programming in a streamlined, fast paced way.

Although born and raised in Cambridgeshire I currently live in the city of Lincoln, a beautiful place that inspires me greatly. Everything about the city makes me feel my happiest and most creative and I try my best to reflect this in my work. I love exploring the city as the calm, charismatic surroundings allow me to work in any way I chose, anywhere I chose. I believe someone truly capable of great work can do it anywhere and like to test my own abilities by working around Lincoln, whether that be in my bedroom, the library, the many coffee shops, or even the green of the great Lincoln Castle. I like dedicating myself to everything I do and hope to demonstrate this in my workflow, and my hobbies. When I'm not attending University, or developing websites I enjoy playing videogames, creating and playing music, wasting time on reddit, and escaping the unescapable abyss that is life by playing with my hamster named Sensei.

For many years before setting my career focus on web development I spent many years persuing a career in Software Developement. I believe this sets me apart as it gave me a great understanding of the best programming practices that are usually undervalued and forgotten in the world of web programming. I also have a large amount of experience designing and devloping both software and websites, including creating and focusing on a User Experience portion of an AR street art app called LandmARk, creating a completely bespoke database management system for a gym using python and SQL, and devloping many weebsites and webapps over the past few years, the majority of which are displayed in the projects section of this page.
